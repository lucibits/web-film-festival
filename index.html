THE EVENT

Every year we choose an amazing venue to screen the best films. The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival (BOFF) is a competitive international film festival showing nothing the best in badass genre film. In addition to the screenings, we have parties, Q&A’s with filmmakers, panels, sponsors and more. The directors of BOFF are committed to celebrating the art of filmmaking and are focused on pushing boundaries to challenge the genre’s preconceptions.
